# Everyone Knows How Much I Love You
**By Kyle McCarthy**

![[content/images/Everyone Knows How Much I Love You.jpg|200]]

> "I saw-as I hadn't before-that behind this smooth woman there was a nerdy girl who had once taken refuge in books." *- Kyle McCarthy, Everyone Knows How Much I Love You*

### Book Review
*Everyone Knows How Much I Love You* by Kyle McCarthy is technically considered a thriller but it felt more like a diary for a character who seems to not quite understand social cues. The book takes you through friendship, jealousy, relationships, and just life. The characters were very realistic in that none of them were perfect or very likeable overall. If I am being honest this book was hard to read as I could not understand where the story was going at times. The randomness in the character development is what gave it a dear diary feel to it. The ending finally had some suspenseful thriller aspects that made me have some faith for the storyline but then it just ended. I had to reread the ending twice to understand that it was truly the ending and I was not getting more to the story, it confused me a bit but from the rest of the book what could I truly expect.
