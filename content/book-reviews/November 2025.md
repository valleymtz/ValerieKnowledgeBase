**By Nicholas Sparks, M. Night Shyamalan**

![[content/images/Remain.jpg|200]]

> "More than anything, she taught me to believe in the idea that love will find me when I least expect it, but that it’s up to me to be ready to receive it." *- Nicholas Sparks & M. Night Shyamalan, Remain: A Supernatural Love Story*

### Book Review
*Remain* by Nicholas Sparks together with M. Night Shyamalan was a suspenseful romance book unlike most. With two well-known writers I had nothing but high hopes for this book. The story takes us through the love story of two characters, (minor spoiler alert, we learn it early in the book) one of which may or may not be living. The book is an emotional love story about loss, love, and moving on. The tragedies that each character must overcome and heal from in the story made you care for the characters and their development. Even the lives and personalities of the supporting characters added to the realistic views of the book and made you root for their happiness. There were unexpected twists, a few scary moments, and a specific awkward love scene that I am not sure how I feel about. Overall, it was a great read that keeps you invested till the very last page.
