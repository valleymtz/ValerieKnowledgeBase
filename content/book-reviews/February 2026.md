# A Love Letter to Whiskey
**By Kandi Steiner**

![[content/images/A Love Letter to Whiskey.jpg|100]]

> "Sometimes we’re more terrified of the good things in life than we are of the bad. We feel we don’t deserve them, or that they aren’t real, that they’ll disappear quickly and easily and we’ll be left in the ruins." *- Kandi Steiner, A Love Letter to Whiskey*

### Book Review
*A Love Letter to Whiskey* by Kandi Steiner is a romance book that truly embodies finding the right man or woman at the wrong time. The book takes you through the point of views of two main characters, a man and a woman, and their story of finding love but never at the right time. Their journey was truly frustrating at times as I felt it was just their stubbornness getting in the way of them finally being together. The book will take you through a rollercoaster of emotions as you follow along their storyline but the growth and the development in the characters made so much sense to the timing of the book. Truly this book is filled with missed opportunities that could have made it a shorter book, but what fun would that be. The twists and turns made it unpredictable but that is what makes it so realistic, the ending is definitely worth the wait.
