# The Women
**By Kristin Hannah**

![[The Women.jpg|100]]

> "The women had a story to tell, even if the world wasn’t quite yet ready to hear it, and their story began with three simple words. We were there." *- Kristin Hannah, The Women*

### Book Review
*The Women* by Kristin Hannah is such a beautifully written historical fiction book about women empowerment. The main friendships that are gained through the struggles of being frontline in the face of battle without actually fighting in the Vietnam war reminds me of the idea of being trauma bonded. This book takes you from a woman’s experience at war, then to her struggles of being home as a woman veteran, and how she overcomes these struggles to help other women with similar stories. Kristin Hannah writes in a way that allows you to be put in the characters shoes and live her story through their eyes. The book portrays courage, friendships, love, betrayal, disappointment, and so much more. Women in Vietnam is not a story often told and therefore this book was a book that was well worth the read to understand that they were there and they were a part of history that should not have been overlooked.
