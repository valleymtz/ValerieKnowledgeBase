# The Coworker
**By Freida McFadden**

![[../images/The Coworker.jpg|200]]

> "Whatever else you can fake, it’s hard to fake being a genuinely kind person. It’s also exceedingly rare." *- Freida McFadden, The Coworker*

### Book Review
*The Coworker* by Freida McFadden is a suspenseful thriller that had me guessing until the very end. Each character is so flawed that when reading along you do not know who to believe or who to root for. The story takes you through unexpected turns leading to an even more shocking ending. The lies and betrayal in this story make you question even your own ethics. The setting made the story that much more believable because honestly do you truly know who your coworkers are and what they go through or have been through. Most of us spend most of our time at work rather than we do in our own homes and our coworkers can truly betray us. Overall, this book is a suspenseful and entertaining read that any thrill seeker will enjoy. Just be sure to read all the way to the end, do not skip the epilogue.
